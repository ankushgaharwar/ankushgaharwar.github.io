Rooted in the rituals of gathering

Founder and ceramicist Naomi Palmer began shaping clay as a way to slow down and savor meals with friends. Today, Palmer Dinnerware is still produced in limited runs, using sustainably sourced stoneware and glazes mixed in-house.

Every collection begins with sketches inspired by the changing Pacific Northwest seasons. From there, prototypes are tested at the studio table—the heart of our design process—to ensure each piece stacks, serves, and delights.

Naomi Palmer Founder & Ceramicist
