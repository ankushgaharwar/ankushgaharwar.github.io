Hi! I'm Ankush. I believe in magic and love. I adore art, football, traveling, and music. I like to cycle and I'm an Amateur Football player, I collect newspapers clippings related to Innovation and Science and I want to see northern lights at least once in a lifetime. I'm a self-taught designer and I have four years of experience in this field. My works seek to make designs that makes a good impact on our society. I think that even a simple design can have a huge impact, like the mine detonator Invented by the Afghan boy and the liter of light project. These are some examples of how someone can improve lives with innovation and a good heart. One thing that I desire is to integrate my design ability and knowledge of architecture, achieving infinite possibilities among different projects. I also believe a good designer should be able to connect with market and industry; therefore I worked in several organizations and took part multiple projects as well as competitions to perfect my skills. I always try to design something new but at the same time think about its effect on society and how it can help in changing life My work on Automated Time Table system was well appreciated in SRM University, one of the biggest university in India.
