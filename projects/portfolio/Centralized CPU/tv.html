Centralized CPU

We all face problems like slow working of laptops and computers,gadates getting outdated within a months, Low memory space. For the past two years I'm trying to create a centralised CPU. A big CPU with which you can connect all your devices via wifi. I want to connect all the computer devices of a house like mobile laptop tablet PC and even Television to a centralized CPU. A powerful CPU that can take the load of all those devises. It will be like the brain of a house. Mobile that also has its own memory and RAM can connect to this CPU via WiFi and this can help in storing data. We can do heavy data work by doing large calculations even from Mobile as it is just working as a screen. This will also be very useful in developing smart houses. The cleaning robots, They security system and everything. All can connect to just one system. And people can also carry a smaller version when they go outside.
